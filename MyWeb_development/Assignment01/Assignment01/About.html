Hotel Mysuru Inn Grand

Nestled away and surrounded by lush green canopies, Country Inn & Suites By Radisson offers elegantly furnished rooms with free Wi-Fi access. The property also houses an outdoor swimming pool and a private nightclub. Guests can enjoy a good workout at the spacious fitness centre. Guests can also treat themselves a traditional massage while a 24-hour front desk can assist guests with laundry/ironing services and room service. Air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a wardrobe and tea/coffee making facilities. Private bathrooms include hot/cold shower facilities and free toiletries.
The hotel houses 2 in-house restaurants known as Mosaic, the all-day dining restaurant, serving Indian, Arabic and Oriental dishes while Spice is the fine dining restaurant that serves authentic flavours of south Indian cuisine.Guests can enjoy cocktails at the poolside bar and grill. Located 10 km away from Mysore Railway and Bus Station, the hotel is also within 12 km away from Mysore Palace and 11 km away from the beautiful Brindavan Garden.
